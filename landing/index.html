Features

Everything you need to
organize your storage.

Just stick a tag on your box, tap it with your phone, and you'll always know what's inside.

📲

Tap to Read

Hold your phone to any labeled box and see exactly what's inside — contents, date packed, and notes.

✏️

Tap to Write

Fresh box to the attic? Tap and type your contents list. Your label updates instantly — no sticky notes.

🎤

Voice Notes

Hands full? Use your voice. Just say "winter coats, size small, two pairs of boots" and it's saved.

🔍

Search Everything

Need the tent? Type it in and find which box it's in. No more pulling down every bin from the attic ladder.

📋

Contents List

Full inventory per box with emoji support. See at a glance what's in storage without lifting a lid.

🏷️

Digital Labels

Reusable NFC tags for every box. Change contents whenever you want. No peeling or rewriting.

Simple Setup

Three steps to an
organized attic.

Takes about 30 seconds per box. You'll wonder why you didn't do it sooner.

1

Stick a Tag

Apply an NFC tag to any box, bin, or tote. They're thin, waterproof, and stick to plastic, cardboard, or wood.

2

Tap & Write

Open the Attic Organizer app, tap your phone to the tag, and type or speak what's inside. That's it.

3

Never Guess Again

Next time you need something, just tap the box with your phone. No dragging bins downstairs to check.

Start organizing today.
